CRITICAL ROLE

The Beginnings

The original cast had been playing a Pathfinder campaign for about two years before they converted their home game into a livestreamed series. The campaign began as a one-off game organized for Liam's birthday. This one-off was played by Liam, Sam Riegel, Laura Bailey, Travis Willingham, Taliesing Jaffe and Orion Acaba, with Matthew Mercer as the DM. Marisha Ray and Ashley Johnson joined the game in later sessions.

In 2015, they were approached by Felicia Day to stream their campaign live on the Geek & Sundry twitch channel. In preparation for the streamed series, Matt converted the game over from Pathfinder to Dungeons & Dragons.

The first campaign and a part of the second campaign were live streamed on the Geek & Sundry Twitch channel. In February 2019, Critical Role parted ways with Geek & Sundry, launched their own Twitch and YouTube channels, and started to self-produce new shows and content under the name Critical Role Productions LLC.

The Campaigns

First Campaign

The first campaign features a group of adventurers called Vox Machina originating from Tal'Dorei. It lasted two and a half years and 115 episodes, the adventures of Vox Machina came to an end on October 12, 2017 in "The Chapter Closes".

Second Campaign

The second campaign started on January 11, 2018 with "Curious Beginnings", and ended three and a hals years and 141 episodes later on June 3rd, 2021 with "Fond Farewells" This campaign began almost 20 years after the end of the Vox Machina campaign and told the story of the Mighty Nein in Wildemount.

Third Campaign

The third campaign started on October 21, 2021 with "The Draw of Destiny" and is currently airing. This campaign begins approximately six years after the end of the Mighty Nein campaign and within a year after the events of Exandria Unlimited, and is set on the continent of Marquet.
